About Us

STROOPIA is a family-owned business dedicated to bringing you the finest Dutch Stroopwafels. Our passion for quality and tradition is baked into every waffle we make. We source the best ingredients to ensure that each bite is a perfect blend of crispy and chewy, with a rich caramel filling that melts in your mouth. It is the rich taste of Amsterdam's finest wafels right into your lap in the center of Cairo's vibrant streets.

Founded in 2016 by a group of friends who shared a love for Dutch culture and cuisine, STROOPIA has quickly become a beloved spot for locals and tourists alike. Our mission is to share the joy of Stroopwafels with our beloved customers all over Egypt. Whether you're enjoying them fresh off the griddle or taking a pack home to share with loved ones, we promise an unforgettable experience with every bite.
